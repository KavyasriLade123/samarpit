Programs

Courses designed for confidence, communication, and employability

Each program combines concept sessions, guided practice, and performance feedback. Learners gain measurable progress in communication fluency, personality development, and professional behavior.

Foundation Soft Skills

Confidence building, communication basics, listening skills, and etiquette.

Duration: 6 weeks

Mode: Classroom + Practical Exercises

Advanced Communication

Public speaking, group discussions, interview communication, and fluency drills.

Duration: 8 weeks

Mode: Mentor-led Workshops

Personality & Career Development

Professional behavior, leadership, presentation readiness, and work culture fit.

Duration: 10 weeks

Mode: Structured Career Lab

Swetcha Offline Program

In-person confidence and communication coaching through practical activities.

Mode: Offline Classroom

Train the Trainer

Designed for educators and mentors to deliver high-impact personality training sessions.

Mode: Workshop + Practice Facilitation

EPS Effective Public Speak

Focused module for stage confidence, speech structure, delivery, and audience engagement.

Mode: Practical Speaking Labs

Young Minds (Offline & Online)

Age-focused communication and personality grooming for school and college learners.

Mode: Offline and Online Batches

Art of Parenting

Guidance for parents on communication, emotional support, and positive child development.

Mode: Interactive Parent Sessions

Program Outcomes

  • Stronger public speaking and one-to-one communication confidence.
  • Improved interview readiness with real-time mock practice.
  • Professional personality with better behavior and teamwork mindset.
  • Industry-oriented communication for internships and job roles.

Future integration: LearnDash can be connected for digital course delivery, progress reports, and member-based access.

Batch Structure

Weekday and weekend batches with beginner-to-advanced progression.

Assessment Model

Entry assessment, module evaluations, and final performance review.
